## Tips

- **Proof points matter most.** "Serving Cromwell since 2009" outranks any slogan.
- **Concrete subject matter drives the design.** "Wool carpet texture, room
  transformations, measurement visits" gives the agent a design signature to work
  with; "quality service" gives it nothing.
- If the client has existing reviews (Google, Facebook), paste two or three good
  ones — real customer language is the best copy source there is.
